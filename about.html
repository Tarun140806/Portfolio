I'm Tarun, a B.Tech student in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science at Shiv Nadar University Chennai. Most of what I do lives on the backend — designing APIs, wiring up databases, and getting retrieval-augmented generation systems to actually retrieve the right thing.

I interned at Indium Software, where I owned the backend of an Internal Engineering Knowledge Search Platform — a RAG system that lets engineers ask natural-language questions about internal codebases and documentation. That meant FastAPI services, JWT auth, multi-tenant RBAC, and a Qdrant-backed retrieval pipeline, working alongside a small team split across agents, coordination, and infra.

I like problems that have a clean underlying structure, whether that's a coding problem or a system design one — which is a big part of why backend and retrieval systems have become my focus.
